Collaboration & Boundaries

This work is collaborative, but structured to protect clarity, momentum, and outcomes.

Your insight into your business, audience, and vision is essential. That input shapes strategic direction. However, the synthesis—the decisions about positioning, hierarchy, visual direction, and structure—is Scott-led.

This approach prevents consensus drift and protects what you're paying for: decisive, coherent systems work. Revisions are limited and clearly defined within each service scope.

If a project requires significantly more back-and-forth than anticipated, we'll discuss scope adjustments transparently before proceeding.
