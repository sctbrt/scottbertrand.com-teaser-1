What's Included

Brand Positioning Review

Value proposition clarity, target audience alignment, competitive differentiation, and overall brand strategy assessment.

Visual Identity Analysis

Logo effectiveness, color palette assessment, typography choices, imagery style, and overall visual consistency across touchpoints.

Messaging Consistency

Tone of voice, brand voice guidelines, key messaging alignment, tagline/positioning statement review, and content consistency.

Touchpoint Audit

Website, social media, email, packaging, and other customer touchpoints for brand consistency and quality.

Competitor Analysis

High-level review of 2-3 direct competitors to identify differentiation opportunities and category positioning.

Prioritized Recommendations

Actionable improvements organized into Now (critical), Next (important), and Later (nice-to-have) categories.

Common Questions

What assets do I need to provide?

Logo files, brand guidelines (if you have them), website URL, and links to any other brand touchpoints (social media, packaging, etc.). I can work with whatever you have available.

Do you provide redesign services?

This audit is assessment and recommendations only. However, if you'd like implementation work after the audit, I offer Brand Reset and other design services.

Can you audit a personal brand?

Yes, this works for both business brands and personal brands.

What if I don't have brand guidelines?

That's fine. I'll assess what you have and can include recommendations for creating brand guidelines in the audit.

What happens after delivery?

You receive the PDF audit and can optionally schedule a 30-minute call to discuss findings. After that, you're free to implement recommendations yourself or engage me for additional work.
