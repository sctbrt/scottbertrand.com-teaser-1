About

Scott Bertrand is a Canadian brand and web systems consultant based in Sudbury, Ontario. He helps small businesses across Northern Ontario and Canada clarify their brand, fix underperforming websites, and build simple digital systems that generate leads.

I work with small businesses, independent founders, local service providers, and companies throughout Northern Ontario. My clients come to me when their brand feels unclear, their website isn't converting, or they need a structured system to replace scattered, ad-hoc approaches.

I'm drawn to the moment where things are almost working, but not quite: the handoffs, the unclear standards, the unowned details. That's where structure becomes leverage.
