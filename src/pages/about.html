About

I'm Scott Bertrand, a brand and web systems consultant based in Greater Sudbury, Ontario. I help businesses bring structure to areas that are almost working—but not quite.

I work with service-based businesses, independent founders, and local organizations across Northern Ontario. Most of my clients come to me at a crossroads: their current brand or website worked well enough to get them here, but it's no longer keeping up with where they're headed.

How I Think

I help small businesses clarify their brand, fix underperforming websites, and build simple digital systems that actually generate leads.

My work is about structure. That means auditing what's already in place, identifying what's working and what isn't, and replacing scattered, ad-hoc efforts with clear systems. I don't build elaborate marketing funnels or chase trends. I focus on clarity, consistency, and conversion: the foundation of any site or brand that needs to work without constant attention.

How I Work

I offer structured services with fixed scope and clear pricing. No hourly billing, no ambiguous retainers. You know what you're getting, what it costs, and what the outcome will be before we start.

Every engagement begins with a review: either a Website Snapshot or Brand Clarity Session. This gives us both a clear picture of what's going on and what to do about it. From there, we decide together if deeper work makes sense.

Background & Perspective

I've spent most of my career inside complex systems: corporate operations, project management, process design. What I've seen, again and again, is how small structural decisions cascade into big problems (or big wins) over time.

I don't see brands or websites as creative expressions. I see them as systems. And like any system, they need architecture. They need intention. They need a point of view.

That perspective shapes everything I do. I'm not interested in what looks good. I'm interested in what works.

Who This Is For

I work best with clients who:

  • Are running a business that already works and want to build on it
  • Prefer direct, honest feedback over polite flattery
  • Value clear systems over vague strategy decks
  • Want a partner who thinks critically, not one who just executes tasks

I work best with clients who value clarity, momentum, and shared responsibility. That's why most engagements start with a review.

Based in Sudbury, Ontario

I live and work in Sudbury, in the heart of Northern Ontario. I serve clients across the region and across Canada, mostly remotely, with a strong preference for businesses that are genuinely rooted somewhere.

Where to Start

If your website isn't converting or your brand feels scattered, start with a quick review. You'll walk away with a clear understanding of what's wrong and a concrete set of priorities to address it, whether we continue working together or not.
