Common Questions

Do you work with businesses outside Sudbury?

Yes. While based in Greater Sudbury, I work remotely with clients across Canada. Local presence means understanding Northern Ontario businesses, not limiting my availability.

What's the difference between an audit and a foundation?

An audit identifies problems and prioritizes fixes. A foundation implements solutions. Most clients start with an audit to understand what needs fixing before committing to larger work.

How long does a typical project take?

Audits: 1-2 weeks. Foundations: 4-6 weeks. Full System: 6-8 weeks. Timelines are clearly defined in each service scope.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for Foundation and Full System projects. 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Audits are paid in full upfront due to the short timeline.

What if I just need small fixes?

Start with an audit. If the scope is truly limited and well-defined, we can discuss targeted à la carte support after the audit clarifies the actual needs.
