What We Cover

Brand Positioning Clarity

Who are you for? What do you stand for? We'll assess whether your positioning is clear, differentiated, and resonant with your target audience.

Voice & Tone Alignment

Does your brand sound like you? We'll evaluate consistency across touchpoints and whether your voice creates the right emotional response.

Visual & Messaging Inconsistencies

Where does your brand feel disjointed? We'll identify gaps between what you say, how you look, and what customers experience.

Direction & Next Steps

You'll leave with clarity on what's holding your brand back and what to prioritize—whether that's messaging, visuals, or deeper brand work.

Common Questions

What should I prepare?

Share your website URL and any key brand materials (logo files, social profiles, marketing materials). The more context, the better—but don't stress about it.

Is this different from a brand audit?

Yes. This is a live strategic conversation—quicker, more interactive, and focused on direction. A Brand Review is a comprehensive written assessment with detailed documentation.

What platform do we use?

Google Meet or Zoom—your choice. You'll receive a calendar invite with the link after booking.

What's in the follow-up email?

A short summary of the key points we discussed—3–5 bullets capturing the main insights and recommended next steps. It's a reference, not a report.

What if I need more after the session?

Many clients use this as a starting point before deeper work. We can discuss next steps at the end of the call—whether that's a full brand audit, brand reset, or something else.
