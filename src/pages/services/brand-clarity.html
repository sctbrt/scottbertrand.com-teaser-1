What We Cover

Brand Positioning Clarity

Who are you for? What do you stand for? I assess whether your positioning is clear, differentiated, and resonant with your target audience.

Voice & Tone Alignment

Does your brand sound like you? I evaluate consistency across touchpoints and whether your voice creates the right emotional response.

Visual & Messaging Inconsistencies

Where does your brand feel disjointed? I identify gaps between what you say, how you look, and what customers experience.

Direction & Recommendations

You'll receive clarity on what's holding your brand back and what to prioritize—whether that's messaging, visuals, or deeper brand work.

Common Questions

What should I prepare?

Share your website URL and any key brand materials (logo files, social profiles, marketing materials). The more context, the better—but don't stress about it.

What format is the deliverable?

You'll receive an annotated PDF (typically 8-10 pages) with examples, callouts, and prioritized recommendations you can reference and share with your team.

Is there a call involved?

No call is required—this is fully async. If you want to discuss the findings, you can add a Phone Consultation ($125) afterward.

How is this different from Strategic Brand Review?

This is a focused assessment of positioning and messaging. The Strategic Brand Review ($550) goes deeper with competitive analysis, market positioning, and expanded recommendations.

What if I need implementation after?

Many clients use this as a starting point before deeper work. The Brand Reset ($2,750) includes full brand identity development with logo, colors, typography, and guidelines.
