Common Questions

What if I have multiple websites?

This service covers one website. For multiple sites, submit separate requests or contact me for bundled pricing.

Do you provide implementation services?

This audit is assessment and recommendations only. However, I'm happy to discuss implementation work separately after the audit is complete.

What size websites do you audit?

This works best for small to medium business websites (5-50 pages). For larger sites, contact me for custom scoping.

Can you audit e-commerce sites?

Yes, though the focus will be on user experience, messaging, and performance rather than platform-specific features.

What happens after delivery?

You receive the PDF audit and can optionally schedule a 30-minute call to discuss findings. After that, you're free to implement recommendations yourself or engage me for additional work.
